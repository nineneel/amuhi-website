AMUHI Forges Strategic Alliance with Ministry of Hajj & Umrah for 2024 Season

Dr. Ahmed Al-Fayed

Director of Communications

calendar_today October 24, 2023
schedule 5 min read

In a landmark move designed to streamline operations for over 500 travel agencies across Indonesia, AMUHI has officially entered into a strategic partnership with regulatory bodies to enhance the pilgrim experience.

The agreement, signed yesterday in Jakarta, marks a significant turning point for the industry. With the resumption of full-quota pilgrimages, the need for a cohesive, transparent, and digitally integrated system has never been more critical. AMUHI, representing the millennial wave of industry professionals, is at the forefront of this digital transformation.

Key Objectives of the Partnership

The collaboration focuses on three primary pillars that directly impact the operational efficiency of member agencies and the safety of pilgrims:

  • check_circle Digital Integration: Unifying visa processing systems with the AMUHI App.
  • check_circle Standardized Training: Mandatory certification for tour leaders through AMUHI Academy.
  • check_circle Crisis Management: A direct hotline establishment for emergency support in Makkah and Madinah.

"This is not just an agreement on paper; it is a commitment to the millions of pilgrims who trust our members with their spiritual journey."

– Mochamad Irfan Yusuf, Minister of Hajj & Umrah RI

Moving forward, all AMUHI members will gain early access to the new quota allocation system starting next month. This exclusive benefit is expected to reduce wait times significantly and provide more certainty for planning itineraries.

What This Means for Members

Existing members need not take any immediate action; the integration will roll out automatically across the AMUHI dashboard. New applicants, however, will now face a more rigorous vetting process to ensure compliance with the elevated standards set forth by this new alliance.
