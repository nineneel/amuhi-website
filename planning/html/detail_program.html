Program Curriculum

Designed by industry veterans and regulatory experts, our curriculum ensures you are compliant, competitive, and customer-centric.

MODULE 1 • WEEK 1-2

Regulatory Compliance & Licensing

Understanding the latest regulations from the Ministry of Hajj & Umrah, visa processing systems, and legal requirements.

MODULE 2 • WEEK 3-4

Operational Excellence & Logistics

Best practices in itinerary planning, accommodation management, transportation logistics, and crisis management on the ground.

MODULE 3 • WEEK 5-6

Digital Marketing & Customer Experience

Leveraging digital platforms (AMUHI Digital) for sales, building trust with pilgrims, and ensuring post-journey satisfaction.
